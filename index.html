Zhiqi Li (李治琦)

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Engineering at Westlake University, jointly with Zhejiang University (ZJU), under the supervision of Prof. Peidong Liu. Prior to this, I earned my Bachelor’s degree from Shanghai JiaoTong University (SJTU) in 2022.

My research interests center on core applications within the field of 3D vision, including 3D Content Generation, Feedforward 3D Reconstruction, World Models. I am particularly passionate about developing efficient and scalable algorithms that bridge the gap between digital representation and real-world understanding, with the aim of enabling more intelligent and interactive 3D systems.

I am always eager to exchange ideas and explore potential collaborations. Please feel free to reach out if you are interested in my work or have related research topics to discuss.
